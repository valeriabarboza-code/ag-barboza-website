Our Story

A Family Built on
Trust & Craft

AG Barboza Corporation was founded by Anthony Barboza, a man who came to South Florida from Peru with a dream and the discipline to see it through. Taught by a trusted friend, Anthony discovered not just a profession — but a calling. Since 2008, his work has spoken for itself, growing entirely through the referrals of families who trusted him in their homes and never looked back.

Anthony built this company on a simple foundation: do the work right, treat every client like family, and never compromise on quality. For 18+ years, that commitment has earned him something no advertisement can buy — a reputation that spreads entirely by word of mouth, one satisfied homeowner at a time. In an industry where trust is hard to find, AG Barboza Corporation has built its name on never cutting corners.

"Integrity is everything. If you lose it, you lose everything. That will never happen here." Anthony Barboza Founder & Master Craftsman

What We Do

Complete Interior Renovation

If it lives inside your home, we can transform it. Every project begins with a free estimate — no commitment, no pressure, just honesty.

Bathroom Renovation

Our signature specialty. From master suites to guest baths — complete gut-and-rebuild with porcelain tile, jacuzzi installation, shower pans, plumbing, and lighting. Every detail, done right.

Most Requested

Kitchen Remodeling

Full kitchen transformations — cabinetry, countertops, backsplash tile, plumbing adjustments, and lighting. We scope each project based on your vision and material choices.

Flooring

From marble and porcelain to laminate and hardwood. We install flooring that complements the full aesthetic of your home, with pricing scaled to square footage and material complexity.

Painting & Finishes

Interior and exterior painting and custom finishes that bring the whole space together — clean lines, smooth coats, and a flawless result that makes every room feel brand new.

Space Conversions

Turn your garage into a livable room, an office, or additional living space. Full build-out including framing, drywall, electrical, flooring, and finishing.

Plumbing & Electrical

All renovation work includes professional plumbing and electrical as needed — light fixture replacement, pipe installation and adjustment, and full system integration.

Good to Know

Questions, Answered

Do you offer free estimates?
Always. Every project starts with a free, no-obligation estimate. We visit your home, review the full scope of the work, and give you an honest, detailed quote before you commit to anything.
What areas do you serve?
We serve homeowners across South Florida, with most of our work in Broward County and Miami-Dade. If you're in the region and not sure whether we cover your area, just ask.
Do you speak Spanish?
Yes. We serve English- and Spanish-speaking families with the same care, so you'll always understand exactly what's happening in your home. Hablamos español.
What kinds of projects do you take on?
Bathrooms are our specialty, but we handle complete interior renovations — kitchens, flooring, painting and finishes, garage and space conversions, and the plumbing and electrical work that goes with them.
How does payment work?
It's simple and transparent: 50% to start, 25% at the midpoint, and 25% when the work is complete and you're happy with it. Everything is spelled out in a written contract before we begin.
How do we get started?
Call or message us and we'll schedule your free estimate at a time that works for you. From there, we put everything in writing — no surprises, no pressure, just clarity.
